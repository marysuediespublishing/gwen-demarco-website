# The Gutter Shrike

The second book in the **Kingdom of Erishum Trilogy** raises the stakes as our hero rises from the mud to face even greater challenges.

## Synopsis

The Gutter Shrike is what they call those who dare to rise above their station. It's meant as an insult, a reminder that no matter how high you climb, you'll always be scum from the gutters. But sometimes, the view from the bottom gives you the clearest sight of the rot at the top.

As the kingdom's foundations begin to crack and ancient powers stir in the darkness, unlikely alliances must be forged. But in a world where trust can get you killed, knowing who to believe might be the deadliest challenge of all.
