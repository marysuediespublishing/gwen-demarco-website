## Author Information

Gwen DeMarco writes paranormal romance novels featuring snarky heroines and grumpy male leads. Her stories blend supernatural creatures, urban fantasy settings, and found family themes with steamy romance and plenty of humor.

## Brand Promise

The core brand promise is "Paranormal romance with a focus on the weird and wonderful" - this differentiates Gwen's work by emphasizing:

- **Snarky heroines** who aren't afraid to speak their minds
- **Grumpy male leads** with hidden hearts of gold
- **Found family themes** that warm the heart
- **Urban fantasy settings** filled with supernatural creatures
- **The weird and wonderful** - embracing the unusual and magical

## Book Catalog

### Sophie Feegle Series (Complete - 5 books + Box Set)
Urban fantasy set in San Francisco with shifters, ogres, and supernatural creatures.

### Kingdom of Erishum Trilogy (Complete - 3 books)
Epic fantasy featuring the mysterious Hyvas creatures and richly developed world.

### Auras & Embers Series (Ongoing - 2 books)
Urban fantasy featuring Auramancers and Bennu Shifters with a fresh take on supernatural powers.
